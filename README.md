A small timer app on a RPi controlled by Nuimo:

Commands:
	Swipe up - Start timer
	Swipe down - Stop timer
	Press Button - Display time left
	Rotate right - Increase time
	Rotate left - Decrease time
	On Time expire - send SMS or play a sound
	On Rotate - Display timer
	
	Time increments in minutes.  Minute range from 1 to 60.  After 60 go up in 30 minute intervals up to 12 hours.
